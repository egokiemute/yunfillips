Our School Structure

Building Strong Foundations for the Future

At Yunfillips Montessori Schools, we provide a well-structured learning journey that aligns with the Nigerian school system while integrating global standards. From the early years through senior secondary, every stage is designed to nurture academic excellence, character, and leadership.

Nursery / Early Years

A nurturing environment where children build social, emotional, and foundational learning skills through play and guided activities.

Primary Education

Focused on literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills, preparing pupils with a strong academic foundation.

Junior Secondary (JSS)

Broad-based subjects designed to strengthen core knowledge and introduce students to science, arts, and technology.

Senior Secondary (SSS)

Prepares students for national and international examinations while developing leadership, innovation, and lifelong learning skills.

The Story of Yunfillips

The name Yunfillips is a combination of “Young” (the founder’s name) and “Philips” (his father’s name), symbolising the family’s commitment to excellence and service.

Founded by Young Eruenah, an experienced teacher with over 30 years in the profession—most of them in the United Kingdom—the school was built on a vision to integrate the best of British and Nigerian educational systems.

Originally established in 1993 as Yunfillips Limited, a support services company in the oil and gas sector, the founder later transitioned the name into education, continuing his lifelong passion for teaching and nurturing children.
