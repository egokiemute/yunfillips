About Our School

A Few Words About Yunfillips Montessori Schools

At Yunfillips Montessori Schools, we are dedicated to nurturing and raising world-class leaders. We combine academic excellence with moral guidance to ensure that every child grows into a confident, responsible, and successful individual.

Vision: To nurture and produce world-class leaders.
Mission: We are committed to delivering an exceptional educational experience that prioritises academic excellence and the personal growth of every student.
Core Value: Academic and Moral Excellence.

Our Story

The name Yunfillips is a blend of “Young” (our founder’s name) and “Philips” (his father’s name), symbolising a legacy of family and excellence. Established in 1993 as Yunfillips Limited in the oil and gas sector, the vision evolved when our founder, an experienced teacher with over 30 years of teaching (including in the UK), transformed it into an educational institution.

Today, Yunfillips Montessori Schools proudly integrates the strengths of both the British and Nigerian educational systems — giving every student a well-rounded, globally relevant education.
